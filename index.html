Mythics Lore

Once upon a time, the matter and raw power known as Chaos created and ruled over the Universe. Over time, Chaos birthed 5 gods, the Primordials. 

The Primordials represented the fundamental forces of nature. As millennia passed, the Primordials found the need for powerful gods to rule the vast macrocosm. Thus, the Titans were created and with them brought about the Golden era.
